Isabel Morais


Isabel Morais grew up in Union, New Jersey, attending Union public schools her whole life. Ever since she was little, Isabel has had a drive to do better with herself. From ages 4 to 14, Isabel took TaeKwonDo, earning the rank of 3rd degree black belt and a 1st degree black belt in Haidong Gumdo, a Korean martial art focued on the sword. She would attend classes five to six times a week. When Isabel turned 14, she made the decision to leave TaeKwonDo to focus on her studies.

Isabel Morais will be a senior at Kean University this upcoming year, graduating in May 2019. She will be graduating with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics.
